Kuakata beach is a beach in southern part of Bangladesh located in Kalapara upazila of Patuakhali district. This beach is 18 kilometers (11 mi) long and 3 kilometers (1.9 mi) wide.[1] The specialty of this beach is that due to its geographical location, sunrise and sunset views can be seen from this beach.[2]

The sunset and sunrise of the Bay of Bengal can be seen from the shores of Kuakata's white sand beach. Its other name is Sagarkanya. Various guest birds can be seen at Kuakata beach during winter.
